Dry Oar Rafting

Our mission at Dry Oar Rafting is to create unforgettable river adventures. We are passionate about sharing the thrill of the rapids while ensuring the highest standards of safety with our expert guides. Join us to connect with nature and forge memories that will last a lifetime.

History

It all started with a simple love for the river. Our founder spent years guiding trips before deciding to build a company that put the guest experience first. Since 2010, we've been dedicated to creating small-group adventures that feel less like a tour and more like a day on the water with good friends.
